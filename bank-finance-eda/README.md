# Bank Finance Data Exploratory Analysis

## Project Overview
This project performs **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on bank finance data to understand data structure, distributions, and key patterns.
The analysis aims to build a solid foundation for further financial analysis and data-driven decision making.

---

## Business Context
In banking and finance, understanding data behavior is critical before applying predictive models or making business decisions.

This project focuses on answering:
- What are the key characteristics of the bank finance data?
- Are there noticeable patterns or anomalies?
- Which variables may require further investigation?

---

## Objectives
- Explore and understand bank finance data
- Identify distributions, trends, and potential outliers
- Generate meaningful insights from a financial perspective
- Prepare the data for potential future modeling

---

## Methods & Techniques
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Descriptive statistics
- Data visualization
- Insight interpretation
